A1:
A GC percentage is a stability factor for any DNA molecule. GC are connected via 3 hydrogen bonds and they require more energy to denaturate and separate,
thus, a high GC% can tell scientists how stable is a region, mutation-wise, or how this strand should be properly manipulated.
