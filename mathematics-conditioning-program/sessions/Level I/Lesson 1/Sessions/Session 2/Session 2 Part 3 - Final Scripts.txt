```python
x = 12

is_twelve = x == 12
is_greater_than_five = x > 5
is_nonzero = x != 0
condition = x > 5

print(is_twelve)
print(is_greater_than_five)
print(is_nonzero)
print(condition)
```

Locked distinction:
- In mathematics, `x = 12` states equality or a condition.
- In Python, `x = 12` assigns.
- In Python, `x == 12` compares.
