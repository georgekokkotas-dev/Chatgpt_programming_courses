# MCP Curriculum Map

## Level I — Basic

1. Mathematical language
2. Numbers, operations, symbolic discipline
3. Fractions, ratios, percentages, scientific notation
4. Powers, roots, logarithm preparation
5. Algebraic expressions and transformations
6. Equations, inequalities, checking
7. Geometry, coordinates, distance, slope, area, volume
8. Variables, units, assumptions, basic modelling

## Level II — Philosophy

1. Mathematical statements
2. Logic, implication, equivalence, negation
3. Quantifiers and counterexample
4. Sets, subsets, operations, indexed families
5. Relations, order, equivalence
6. Functions
7. Proof methods
8. Proof writing

## Level III — Moderate

1. Sequences and limits
2. Functions, continuity, local behaviour
3. Derivatives and rates
4. Integrals and accumulation
5. Vectors and analytic geometry
6. Matrices, systems, linear transformations
7. Probability, random variables, distributions
8. Statistics, inference, uncertainty, biological data

## Level IV — High

1. Differential equations
2. Stability, equilibria, phase portraits
3. Numerical methods and error
4. Scientific computing
5. Optimization and parameter estimation
6. Abstract algebra
7. Stochastic/statistical modelling
8. Validation, sensitivity, interpretation

## Level V — Advanced

1. Metric spaces
2. Topology
3. Advanced analysis
4. Manifolds and differential geometry
5. Tensors and coordinate changes
6. Nonlinear dynamics
7. Advanced optimization and variational thinking
8. Higher structures

## Level VI — Research Grade

1. Mathematical biology
2. Genomic modelling and genetic influence dynamics
3. Epidemiological modelling
4. Statistical genetics and bioinformatics mathematics
5. Model construction from assumptions
6. Proof writing for research
7. Reproducible computing and GitHub portfolio
8. Independent research project/report
